cancel culture

noun

1. When the collective media joins together to call someone out for offensive actions or statements.

Image taken from Pixabay.

These days, it's easy to do a Google search or use Twitter history to find dirt on people. As Gen Z becomes more familiar with using technology, we find more ways to use it for good - and unfortunately - bad.

The other side of the problem lies with those being "cancelled". Remember those digital footprint lessons from elementary school? Use them. If you have to question it, don't post it!

The most important step to creating a functional society is education. Please take the time to educate yourself on topics that are typically seen as "uncomfortable" to talk about.
